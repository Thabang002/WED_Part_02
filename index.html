Good day everybody!
BakeryCo is one of the top bakeries in Limpopo. Started in 2014 by identical twin brothers, our cakes are filled, layered, swirled, piped, and drizzled. We love experimenting with new recipes and techniques, making every addition to our cakes and cupcakes—from meringues, curds, sauces, brittles, caramels, and more—fresh every day. We never use artificial flavors or additives and bake exclusively with premium ingredients, including organic free-range eggs, butter, and stoneground unbleached flour, ensuring our cakes stand out above the rest.
